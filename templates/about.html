Who We Are

Skill Tracker is a digital companion built by developers, for developers — designed to help you take charge of your growth journey.

Our Mission

To empower learners and developers by making skill tracking simple, visual, and meaningful.

What We Offer

  • ✅ Track your daily coding hours
  • 📁 Log job applications and interviews
  • 📝 Journal your learning progress and breakthroughs
  • 📊 Visualize your improvement and see how far you’ve come

Why We Built This

We noticed a gap in the productivity tools available for developers — most are either too complex or too generic.

Skill Tracker is our way of solving that problem.
